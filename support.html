ClassQ — live classroom Q&A

Need help? Email us at support@classq.tech and we'll get back to you within 1–2 business days.

Please include the email address on your ClassQ account and, if relevant, the course join code.

Common questions

  • I can't join a course — join codes are 6 characters, entered exactly as your instructor shared them. If the code is rejected, ask your instructor to confirm the course is in the current term.
  • My session isn't accepting questions — sessions open automatically during their scheduled week. If a session shows as past or upcoming, it's read-only.
  • Voice questions aren't transcribing — check that ClassQ has microphone permission in your device settings, and that you're online while recording.
  • Managing your subscription — instructor plans are billed through the App Store or Google Play. Manage or cancel from your device's subscription settings, or from Manage Plan inside the app.
  • More answers — see the FAQ on our homepage.
