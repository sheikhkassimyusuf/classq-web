Questions, answered.

Do students have to pay?

No — students always join free with a course code from their instructor. Only instructors subscribe, for the courses they run.

Does it work for online and hybrid classes?

Yes. ClassQ is independent of your video platform — it works alongside Zoom, Teams, or a physical lecture hall. Any setup where someone is taking questions.

Are questions really anonymous?

Questions are anonymous to other students. Instructors can see who asked, which keeps the room accountable without the social cost of raising a hand.

What stops inappropriate questions?

Every submission is screened by AI before it posts — harassment, hate, and spam are rejected on the spot. Instructors can also hide anything that slips through, and restore it if they change their mind.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are billed through the App Store or Google Play and can be cancelled there anytime — you keep access until the end of the billing period.
